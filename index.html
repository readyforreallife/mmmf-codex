School and Community Implementation Resource

A program for the skills students must live, not just learn.

Modern Manners & Mental Fortitude is a real-world life skills program built for schools, community organizations, and families who want practical tools for communication, regulation, decision-making, and accountability. This repository brings the core materials together in one public-facing home for review, planning, and adoption conversations.

Why It Matters

Students face real decisions before adults ever teach the tools.

MMMF gives facilitators a structured way to teach the behaviors and judgment skills that shape trust, opportunity, and long-term success in classrooms, workplaces, families, and community settings.
